Lodging

Airbnb in Reykjavík

This airbnb was located in downtown Reykjavík, which was very close to various restaurants, grocery stores, and notable landmarks like the church and Sun Voyager. The apartment itself was very homey, with traditional Iceland architecture, making the stay all the more special.

Vík Apartments

Most of the lodging accomodations in Vík are on the pricier side, and this was one of the cheapest ones we found. It was very nice, with a full kitchen, private washer and dryer, and an ocean view. This was my favorite place in terms of lodging.

Guesthouse in Hofn

Like Vík, housing accomodations are also very expensive, but this was our cheapest stay. My family and I were only staying for a night, arriving late, and leaving early in the morning, so we decided to live in a guesthouse, and paid a little extra for a private bathroom. The stay overall was pleasant, and just what we needed for the amount of time we were staying there for.
