Iceland Trip Summer 2021

About Iceland

Iceland is a European country and island that is located in the North Atlantic Ocean. It is home to some of the greatest natural phenomenons like glaciers, volcanoes, hot springs, and beaches.

Capital: Reykjavík

Population: 356,991

Covid-19 Precautions

There are no covid cases in Iceland, and most of our activities were outdoors, so masks were not required anywhere. For the journey there, my entire family is vaccinated so we did not have to take any tests prior to departing – we did have to fill out a pre-registration form online on Iceland's travel website. We were also required to take a test upon landing, and received the results within five hours. While leaving for home, we were required to take a test no more than 72 hours prior to departure, for which we scheduled while we were in Iceland. The process was pretty smooth, and efficient
